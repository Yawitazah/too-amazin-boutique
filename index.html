You Are Amazing

Meet the Founder

Shanae Jeffries

Too Amazin Boutique began with a simple belief — that every woman deserves to feel too amazing to be overlooked.

What started as a love for beautiful jewelry, statement sunglasses, and finishing touches has grown into something bigger: a welcoming boutique in the heart of Rock Hill where style meets community. Shanae created a space that isn't just about what you buy — it's about who you become when you walk through the door.

Beyond the racks of curated accessories, Too Amazin is built to lift others up. The boutique opens its doors to fellow entrepreneurs, giving beauty and boutique businesses a real storefront to share their craft with the public.

Our Mission

To create a beautiful, welcoming space where women feel confident and celebrated — and where rising entrepreneurs find room to grow. We share our home with other dreamers because we believe success is sweeter when there's space at the table for everyone.
